Bread and egg Recipe

Breathtaking Bread and Egg combo

Description:

Making Breand and Egg the Nigerian Way, A wonderful Treat anyone could ever have.

Ingredients

  • Bread(Any type would suffice)
  • Egg or Eggs(This is more advisable for maximum feel)
  • Tomatoes and chilly pepper
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Toaster

Steps Involved:

  1. Get your Bread and Your Toaster ready
  2. Break your eggs and whisk them
  3. slice all your seasonings and pour into the whisked egg
  4. Mix alltogether and spread on the bread
  5. Put the bread into the toaster and wait for 5mins
  6. Redo each step for the slice of breads you want to consume
  7. Thank me later!!
